YU YOUTH UNFILTERED

MISSION

The Youth Unfiltered is dedicated to serving the new generation by making sexual education accessible and approachable. We advocate for sharing essential knowledge that students need to navigate their lives—including understanding their sexual desires—and help them address their curiosity with thoughtful guidance and accurate information. We strengthen conservative values by equipping young people to define their boundaries and make informed choices, preventing the risks that may arise when curiosity is left unguided and unaddressed.
